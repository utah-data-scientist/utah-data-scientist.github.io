General advice

Keep in mind as you do online research (including here) that there is no one ordained way to get in the field. There is a plethora of sources out there, and as you scour keep in mind this principle (borrowing from Tolstoy):

“All [successfull approaches to getting into data science] are alike; each [unsuccessful approach to getting into data science] is [unsuccesful] in its own way.”

This just means that good advice will have similar themes, like taking the initiative and reading a book, complete a challenge, participate at local meetups, etc.

Here are a few good ones to get you started:

  • Advice For New and Junior Data Scientists. Of course advice is just advice, but this guy’s advice got almost 10k claps on Medium, so it must be good right?!
  • First Data Science Job by Reshama Shaikh. I had the privilege of meeting Reshama when she was a TA at Metis and she’s super knowledgeable. Definitely check this out.
  • Build your Data Science Portfolio
  • 70+ Resources for Transitioning to a Data Science Career. Settle in for this one because it’s a lot but Mode has done a great job of collecting these sources. Be sure to check out the Tales from the transition trenches section.
